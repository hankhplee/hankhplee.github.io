My name is Hank Lee, and my Chinese name is Hao-Ping Lee (李浩平). I’m an incoming PhD student (Fall 2021) at the SPUD Lab led by Sauvik Das. My current research interests are at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction, Ubiquitous Computing, and Usable Privacy and Security.
I’m currently visiting at National Taiwan University working with Shang-Tse Chen. I also work with Yung-Ju (Stanley) Chang as a Research Assistant at National Chiao Tung University, where I earned my bachelor’s degree in computer science in 2018. I was a Visiting Researcher at the School of Computing and Information Systems at the University of Melbourne advised by Tilman Dingler in 2019.
Research Interests: Human-Computer Interaction, Ubiquitous Computing, Usable Privacy & Security, Computer-Mediated Communication
