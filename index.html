My name is Hank Lee, and my Chinese name is Hao-Ping Lee (李浩平). I’m an incoming PhD student (2021 Fall) at the SPUD Lab led by Sauvik Das. My current research interests are at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction, Usable Privacy and Security, and Adversarial Machine Learning.
Due to the pandemic, I’m currently visiting at National Taiwan University working with Shang-Tse Chen. I also work as a Research Assistant advised by Yung-Ju (Stanley) Chang at National Chiao Tung University, where I also earned my bachelor’s degree in computer science in 2018. I was a Visiting Researcher at the School of Computing and Information Systems at the University of Melbourne under the supervision of Tilman Dingler in 2019.
Research Interests: Human Computer Interaction, Ubiquitous Computing, Usable Privacy & Security, Computer Mediated Communication
